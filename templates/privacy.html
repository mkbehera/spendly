Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects the financial information you manually enter (expenses, income, categories, dates, descriptions) and the minimal account information required for authentication (email address). No unnecessary personal data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power the expense‑tracking features of the Service: calculating totals, generating reports, and displaying visualizations. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the server that runs Spendly. It is protected with standard security measures (encrypted at rest where possible, access‑controlled environments). You are encouraged to back up your data regularly.

4. Third‑Party Services

Spendly may integrate with third‑party services (e.g., email providers for password reset). These services are only used to facilitate core functionality, and they receive only the minimum information required. We do not share your financial data with them.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
